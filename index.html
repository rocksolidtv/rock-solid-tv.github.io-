About Rock Solid TV

Welcome to Rock Solid TV — where truth doesn’t ask for permission.

This isn’t your average YouTube channel. This is uncensored history, unfiltered truth, and unshakable faith carved in stone. Founded by Rocky Smith, a Bible-believing truther from West Tennessee with a fire in his bones and a mic in his hand, Rock Solid TV digs where others won’t, asks what others fear, and says what others silence.

Raised in a strong, Bible-based Christian home, Rocky memorized entire chapters of the King James Bible before most kids knew their multiplication tables. At age 7, he wrote his own sermon, recorded it on cassette, and handed it to his grandfather — a local AM radio preacher. That sermon aired for years. Why? Because even at seven, Rocky wasn’t playing church.

He’s a 25-year survivor of leukemia and a bone marrow transplant warrior. When the doctors wrote him off, God wrote him back in. Psalm 118:17 became his war cry: “I shall not die, but live, and declare the works of the LORD.”

That’s what this channel is — declaration. Not of opinions. Of facts, Scripture, and hard truths they buried deep.
